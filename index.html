About Me

I am a graduate of UNC - Chapel Hill with majors in Political Science and Spanish language. Following college, I have worked as a customer service representative, warehouse supervisor, project manager and personal banker.

I am currently enrolled in UNCC's Coding Bootcamp, with the hope of using the skills I learn there to become a full-stack web developer. I enjoy the creative outlet of coding, as well as the mathematical nature and problem-solving orientation of the industry.

On a personal level, I am a proud husband and father to our 1-year old son. In my free time, I enjoy watching him learn to walk and talk, listening to music, and watching my beloved UNC Tar Heels in whatever sport I can find them participating.
