About Me

With almost 10 years of professional experience across a wide array of roles and industries, I am a recent graduate of UNCC's Full Stack Web Development bootcamp.


Over the course of that 12-week fulltime bootcamp, I have gained experience in a wide array of programming languages and technologies. I have worked in HTML5/CSS3, Javascript, JQuery, AJAX/API, Node.js, Express, SQL, MySQL, Sequelize, MongoDB, Mongoose, React.js and various CSS frameworks.


Since graduation, I am working as a freelance developer with a diverse pool of projects including a retail site, a movie promotional site, and a real estate listing site. I love the freedom and creative license that comes with freelance development, but I am ultimately seeking a full-time position as a Junior Developer. I love web design, but I am also very interested in server-side coding and database management. Truly, I am passionate about all aspects of web development
